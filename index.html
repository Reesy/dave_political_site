David Isaac is a dedicated Lee College graduate, successful business owner, and proven community advocate running for Lee College Board. With a strong track record of protecting taxpayer interests and demanding accountability, David brings real-world experience and fiscal responsibility to serve our community.

As a local business owner and active community member, David understands the importance of responsible spending and transparent governance. He's running to ensure that every tax dollar is spent wisely and that Lee College delivers real value for both students and taxpayers.

  • Proven fiscal watchdog & taxpayer advocate
  • Successful business owner & Lee College graduate
  • Champion for transparency & accountability
  • Advocate for practical skills & workforce readiness

"I believe in fiscal responsibility, transparent governance, and ensuring Lee College delivers real value for both students and taxpayers. Together, we can protect your tax dollars while building a stronger educational foundation for Baytown's future."

— David Isaac

MY COMMITMENTS

A Vision for Fiscal Responsibility

Stopping Tax Increases

Fighting to protect your wallet and ensure responsible fiscal management.

  • Vote NO on unnecessary tax increases
  • Use surplus funds for taxpayer relief
  • Attract international students for revenue
  • Cut wasteful administrative spending

Real Accountability

Bringing transparency to how your tax dollars are spent.

  • Record and publish all board meetings
  • Establish strong ethics oversight
  • Add taxpayer oversight to committees
  • Track record of protecting taxpayers

Practical Education

Focusing on workforce readiness and valuable skills.

  • Measure & improve student services value
  • Fair contribution from all students
  • Prioritize job skills & technical training
  • Support entrepreneurship programs
