Sarah Johnson is a passionate educator, dedicated parent, and tireless community advocate running for School Board in District 5. With over 15 years of classroom and administrative experience, Sarah brings a unique perspective shaped by both professional expertise and personal investment in our schools.

As a mother of three children currently enrolled in District 5 schools, Sarah understands the daily realities families face. She's running to ensure that every child, regardless of background or neighborhood, has access to:

  • High-quality instruction from supported teachers
  • Safe and welcoming school environments
  • 21st century learning resources and technology
  • Comprehensive mental health support

"Our schools should be places where every child feels valued, challenged, and prepared for the future. With your support, I'll bring my experience, energy, and collaborative spirit to the School Board to make this vision a reality."

— Sarah Johnson

MY COMMITMENTS

A Vision for Educational Excellence

Teacher Support

Increase teacher pay, reduce class sizes, and provide more professional development opportunities.

  • Competitive salaries to attract and retain talent
  • Strengthen mentoring programs
  • Class size cap of 22 students

Mental Health

Expand mental health services to support student wellbeing and academic success.

  • 1 counselor per 250 students ratio
  • Trauma-informed training for staff
  • Partnerships with community providers

Technology

Bridging the digital divide and preparing students for a technology-driven world.

  • 1:1 device access for all students
  • Digital literacy curriculum
  • Affordable home internet options

Equity

Creating schools where every child has the opportunity to thrive.

  • Universal pre-K access
  • Bias training for staff
  • Cultural competency standards

Curriculum

Developing rigorous, relevant learning experiences for all students.

  • Expand STEM/STEAM programs
  • Diverse literature in all grades
  • Project-based learning initiatives

Community

Engaging families and community partners in public education.

  • Multilingual family communication
  • Parent leadership academies
  • Expanded volunteer programs
