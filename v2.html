My Platform

Three key priorities for a better Lee College and Baytown community

Transparency & Accountability

  • Push for video-recorded board meetings and public access to committee meetings
  • Establish city-wide listening sessions like City Hall
  • Create an ethics committee to ensure proper oversight
  • Monthly public broadcasts of key decisions and discussions

Tax Relief

  • Oppose unnecessary tax increases (voted against the 1.25% increase)
  • Use college surplus funds to offer tax relief to residents
  • Promote international student enrollment to generate revenue
  • Responsible budgeting to prevent unnecessary tuition or tax hikes

Student Opportunities

  • Increase student services spending (currently only 4% of budget)
  • Update student government funding (stagnant since 2006)
  • Reinstate the Student Leadership Council for direct student input
  • Support entrepreneurship programs and faculty research

About David Isick

A dedicated community advocate, business owner, and Lee College graduate running to bring transparency, tax relief, and student opportunities to the Lee College Board of Regents.

Community Advocate

Successfully advocated for the reinstatement of Baytown's ethics commission after a four-year battle.

Taxpayer Champion

Helped recover $3.25 million for taxpayers from a faulty contract with the San Jacinto Mall.

Education Focused

Lee College graduate with a Certificate of Leadership in the Public Sector from UHCL.

Global Perspective

Speaks Japanese and has international business experience to attract global opportunities for Lee College.
