About David Isick

As a Lee College graduate, business owner, and long-time community advocate, I understand the challenges facing our college and our taxpayers. I've spent years fighting for fiscal responsibility and transparency in Baytown.

My opponent voted for the recent tax increase, but I would have stood firm against it. With surplus funds available, we should be looking at tax relief, not tax hikes.

My unique background—including speaking Japanese—positions me to attract international students who can bring outside money to support our college without burdening local taxpayers.

"I don't just talk about accountability—I achieve it. My advocacy recovered $3.25 million for taxpayers from the flawed San Jacinto Mall deal. I'll bring that same determination to the Lee College Board."
